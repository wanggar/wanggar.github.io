    • NBA Fortune

    NBA Fortune is a gamified investment app that allows users to trade NBA player stocks. In this app, each active NBA player is represented as a stock. The stock price is a function of plaers'individual on-court performance and team overall win/loss.

    Background

    During my internship at NBA, a fellow intern named Stan and I came up with this app idea. We believed that the app could offer NBA fans an exciting digital experience. It would make the fans stay more engaged with the NBA season as they make investments with virtual currency.

    After I returned back to school in fall, I shared this app idea with three friends from my iOS App Development class and invited them to build the app with me. None of us had previous iOS development experience. Amie and Brian majored in communication design. Shane, a graduate CS student, studied system engineering at his undergraduate school. As product development newbies, we did not know how challenging the project was going to become until we dove in.

    Functions & Capability

    - A homehub page that displays most traded player stocks and NBA latest news.

    - A portfolio carousel that stores currently owned player stocks

    - Stock prices fluctuate based on an algorithm that mathematically factors in playersâ€™ recent performance.

    - User can search for any NBA players in the database.

    - Real-time price and portfolio update

    Front-end Design

    Color Spec
    Type Face
    Gill Sans
    Prototyping Tool
    Adobe XD

    NBA Fortune is a data-heavy game app. It has information on game statistics, stock price, transaction history, share volume, etc. Users could feel inundated with all those data. Therefore, making the app clean and easy to read was the main design principle.

    We came up wtih two designs. The design on the left side is more visual while the one on the right has a more sleek look. We eventually chose the right one because its design is much cleaner. The gradient color makes the user interface look smooth and glossy.

    Back-end Engineering

    Language: Swift
    API: MySportsFeed & NBA RSS
    Database: Firebase

    The software is written in Swift language on XCode. The two main technical challenges are player database management and stock price realtime updates. To get game statistics, the software sends API calls to MySportsFeed and parses returned JSON files. A player data model is implemented so that team members can program with the model-view-controller paradigm and access players' key variables in any swift file within the project. Meanwhile, the software stores and updates players'stock value, share volume, and users'portfolio value in Firebase.

    Besides the above challenges, we also needed to divide the whole project into individual work streams robustly. Since it was my first time leading a product development team, I could have done a better job in breaking down the whole project into individual pieces. Only with an highly efficient division of tasks would the integration process be swift and easy.

    Steering Wheel

    Steering Wheel is a website that curates heartfelt human conversations exchanged during Uber rides.

    Background

    As a frequent user of the app Uber, I had the chance to meet many drivers who have interesting soul and strong character. They come from all walks of life. Among them are prolific artists, obsessive craftsmen, social workers, and undercover multi-millionaires. When they are off the wheel, they produce art and music, fight injustice and diseases, make jewelries, and run personal business.

    Through casual conversation, I learned their amazing life outside of vehicle. The laidback chat gave the digital app Uber some human touch.

    To celebrate the human aspect of Uber, I built Steering Wheel to highlight some of the best conversation exchanges on the road.

    Content

    The website features multi-media original blog posts and videos. The blog posts cover mini-content series such as driver biography, conversation takeaways, and exclusive interviews. The intent of the blog posts is to celebrate human stories of everyday people like Uber drivers. Meanwhile, the blog provides a space for readers to muse on the meaning of life as they observe life journey of others.

    In addition to blog posts, the website also hosts an original short-documentary that features the entrepreneurial paths of three Singaporean Uberpreneurs - Uber drivers who are also entrepreneurs.

    To summarize, the whole site features the following content franchises:

    1. Driver Biography
    2. Conversation Musing
    3. Interviews with the Driver
    4. UberPreneurs

    Impact

    As I continued to talk to my Uber drivers and create meaning content, I got the opportunity to present a TED talk hosted by Washington University in St.Louis. Through the talk, I wanted to convey a simple message: In this fast-paced world, we should still preserve our curiosity and take a moment to celebrate the lives around us.

    I was glad that the talk reached audience online, my parents and friends back home in China, teachers and students at my middle school, many others in my own communities. As the talk continues to gain its views, I use this talk to spread the positive message that humanity still endures in the digital era.

    Rikai Labs

    Genie English is a chatbot that teaches Chinese young adults English on Wechat.

    Background

    As English as Second Language (ESL) market is booming in China, English lessons are also becoming more expensive. A private English lesson in the city of Shanghai can cost from 300 to 500 RMB per hour, a price unaffordable for many young English adults that want to learn English to improve their career prospect.

    To make English lessons more accessible and convenient for Chinese young adults, I helped build English Genie when interning at Rikai Labs, an internet startup based in Shanghai.

    Product Development

    As a product development intern, I was responsible for developing interactive lesson content for Genie English. Once an ESL student myself, I emphasized with young adult English learners and designed lessons around their biggest struggle - not having enough time for learning the language.

    As life gets busier and harder in Shanghai, the daily schedule for those young adults is highly fragmented. They rarely have uninterrupted, extended period of time for leisure and self-improvement. Even if they do happen to have small blocks of free time, they usually escape to their social media - WeChat and Instagram.

    Therefore, I decided to design short, memorable English lessons on the Wechat mini-program platform where users can fully engage with the lesson material and repeat learning multiple times a day. The content was made interesting and engaging enough that they would spend at least 8 minutes per session when they are on a subway commute or wait in a line.

    Content

    At the time of the internship, I had a false notion that the only meaningful way to contribute to a digital product is by coding it. Therefore, I felt a bit inadequate because I did not have enough industry coding experience to write software for Genie English. But, developing lesson material for Genie English helped me realize that producing meaningful content can also make users excited about the product.

    To support Genie English, I developed the following integrated content:

    1. texting-based lessons on American popular culture and etiquette
    2. automated chatbot scripting based on usersâ€™ input
    3. gamified quizzes and interactive exercises
    4. customized progress report

    Business Requirement

Also, people often associate watch with time, a metaphsical entity of urgency and importance.
